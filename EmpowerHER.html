EmpowerHER

Donate
Uplifting Women and Girls to thrive and lead with confidence.
In African government secondary schools, female students encounter numerous challenges affecting their education and well-being. Many lack access to vital menstrual hygiene products, risking health issues and absenteeis; Additionally, the absence of mentorship programs contributes to a lack of self-confidence and personal development, creating a significant empowerment gap. Below are how EmpowerHER seeks to break these barriers and solve fundamental challenges;

Education & Literacy:
EmpowerHER focuses on increasing access to education for young girls, aiming to improve literacy rates and provide knowledge and skills to succeed in life.

Health & Hygiene:
The project addresses the lack of access to essential health and hygiene products , such as sanitary pads which can hinder a girl's ability to attend school regularly. We tackle this by provising the essentials which go a long way to solve truancy.

STEM Education:
Recognizing the stark gap in STEM fields, EmpowerHER actively encourages and guides girls into exploring non-traditional and male dominated fields such as Engineering and Technology. The goal is to help girls break the glass ceiling and become a beacon of hope.

"To educate girls is to reduce poverty"

Kofi Annan
Transformative Strategy
Supporting the well-being of teenage girls in Africa is imperative due to the myriad challenges they face. From limited access to education and healthcare to cultural norms restricting their freedoms, these girls often endure early marriage, pregnancy, and gender-based violence.
By extending aid to initiatives addressing their needs, we can tackle crucial areas, enabling these girls to break the cycle of poverty and realize their potential.
