Youth Tech Connect

Fostering Future Innovators:
Step into the future with Youth Tech Connect!
Our goal is clear: to empower the next generation with the knowledge and tools they need to become self-sustaining, digitally savvy individuals. Through interactive sessions, we equip students with valuable insights into online resources, tools, and platforms that can shape their futures. But that's not all - our program is designed to reward dedication and commitment. After each session, some exceptional students are presented with the gift of technology – tablets and laptops – to further enhance their learning experience.
Fundamental Improvement Opportunity
In Africa, many high school students, despite their potential, face significant barriers to success in the digital age. Limited access to the internet and a lack of digital literacy skills are holding them back from seizing the opportunities that the online world offers

The future belongs to the young and we must not only prepare them but empower them to shape it.

Mo Ibrahim
Our Improvement Plan
By providing mentoring and education to 300 high school students each month, we are bridging the digital divide and empowering the youth of Africa. Through this program, we equip promising students with essential digital skills, expose them to valuable online resources, and inspire them to harness the internet for self-sustainability.
